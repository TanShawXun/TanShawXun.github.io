   J.CO


History

J.CO is the fastest growing donut & coffee brand in Indonesia with 236 stores opened since 2005. They have become one of the market leaders for donuts and coffee.In 2017 it already have 275 branch in South East Asia countries such as Malaysia, Singapore, Philippines, and Hong Kong. JCO open it’s first branch in Malaysia in 2007 and when 2017 it already have 17 store in here . In 2008, they open their first branch in Singapore with total 3 stores. In 2012, J.CO enter Philippines, and open 44 stores in 2017. They open their first branch in Hong Kong at December 2018.
